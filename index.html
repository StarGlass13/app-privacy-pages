Privacy Policy — Last updated: June 29, 2026

Overview

Destiny ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your information with third parties except as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information needed to provide the oracle experience:

  • Birth date (month and day) — entered voluntarily during onboarding, stored locally on your device only, used to determine your astrological sign for personalized readings.
  • Oracle reading content — the cards, signs, or runes you draw are sent to Anthropic's API to generate your reading. No personally identifying information is included in these requests.
  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA) — used by Google AdMob to serve relevant ads. You may opt out of personalized ads in your device's privacy settings.

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalized oracle readings powered by AI (via Anthropic's Claude API)
  • To remember your astrological sign across sessions
  • To display advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To improve app stability and performance

Third-Party Services

Destiny uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — generates AI-powered oracle readings. Reading prompts are sent to Anthropic's servers. No personal data beyond the oracle context is included.
  • Google AdMob — serves advertisements. AdMob may collect your device's advertising identifier and usage data to serve relevant ads.

Data Storage

Your birth date and preferences are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device except as part of reading requests (which include only astrological sign data, not your birth date itself).

We do not maintain any servers or databases containing your personal information.

Children's Privacy

Destiny is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Your Rights

  • Delete your data — uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.
  • Opt out of personalized ads — go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising on your iPhone.
  • Contact us — email us at rudnicknicholas1@gmail.com with any privacy concerns.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
