Frequently Asked Questions

I don't see any ads — is that normal?
Ads can take a little time to start showing after install, especially right after launch. If ads never appear after extended play, please email us.

How do I unlock new rocket paint, trails, or pilots?
Earn coins during runs, then spend them in the Hangar (accessible from the main menu or the game-over screen).

Is my progress saved?
Yes — your best score, coins, and unlocked cosmetics are saved locally on your device.
